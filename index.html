A Little About Me

After receiving my B.A. in Design from the University of California, Davis, I started working for a small interior design start-up in Truckee, CA. After a few months working with furniture and interiors, I realized that I really missed the visual and graphic elements of design. This interest in graphics led me towards the world of web design through front-end development and UI/UX design. Now, I am taking courses in front end development in order to strengthen my HTML, CSS, and Javascript skills. I hope to be a successful front-end developer.
